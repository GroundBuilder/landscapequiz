ProvinceQuiz

Welcome

This is a landscape quiz over Swedens 24 different landscape. Every landscape has its own landscape shield.

This quiz is about to learn how the different shields looks like in every landscape in Sweden.

Quiz game rules:

You will see 25 different province shields of sweden.
Your task is to klick on the right province name.
You will get 4 options, only one is right. You can any time reset your score with one click.
At the end you will be able to do it again an add points to your score. Or start over again and reset your points.
